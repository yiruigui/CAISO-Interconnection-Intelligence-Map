Transmission Capacity
Sort by Available Capacity MW
What it shows How much room is left on each transmission constraint before it's full. Large green dot = lots of available capacity.
Calculation Max planned capacity minus what's already allocated (CPUC IRP 2024 Attachment A).
Source CPUC IRP 2024 Attachment A (Aug 2024).
02
Queue Congestion
Sort by Queue-Capacity Ratio
What it shows How crowded each constraint is with developers waiting to connect. Ratio above 1× = more queued MW than line capacity.
Calculation Active solar/wind/storage queue MW ÷ planned constraint capacity.
Source Live CAISO interconnection queue via Gridstatus.io.
03
Arbitrage Opportunity
Sort by LMP Spread
What it shows How much a battery could earn charging cheap and discharging at peak. Wide price swings = more arbitrage revenue.
Calculation Top-4h avg minus bottom-4h avg daily price, trailing 12 months. Scored by CAISO zone (NP15/SP15/ZP26).
Source CAISO day-ahead LMP (OASIS), trailing 12 months.
04
Industrial Heat Clusters
Sort by Heat Demand Proximity
What it shows Factories using high-temp process heat (200–500°C) near each constraint — Antora's target customers.
Calculation EPA industrial CO₂ emissions, filtered to sectors matching Antora's 200–500°C output range.
Source EPA GHGRP · in development.
